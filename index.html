Propeller

The RV-8 comes with a 3-blade propeller as standard, and is able to be equipped with up to 210 HP, giving it a top speed of 220mph.

Cockpit

With two tandem-positioned seats, the RV-8 is assured to give you a fighter-like feel. The front seat sits pilots up to 6'7" with its wide fuselage and comfortable cockpit.
Two baggage compartments (one forward and one aft) means that there is plenty of storage for your cross-country journeys.
